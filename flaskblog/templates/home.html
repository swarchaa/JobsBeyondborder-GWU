What is Jobs Beyond Border?

It is a platform dedicated to the needs of international students. All international students coming to the US should have a dedicated tool that will make them competitive in the US job marketplace. Our platform will do its best to help in job search, educate with content and provide advanced analysis tools so that users can slice and dice H1B data.


Our role

How do we do it?

At Jobs Beyond Border, we believe in empowering the international students community and ensuring that your job search is targeted and effective. We provide you with pre-filtered jobs, educative blogs and a complete real-time analysis of the US job market, so that you can make the right decisions at the right time.
